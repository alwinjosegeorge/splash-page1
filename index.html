Mozilla is a global non-profit organization known for developing the Firefox web browser , which prioritizes user privacy and open-source technology. It was founded in 1998 to promote an open and accessible internet for everyone. Mozilla also develops projects like Thunderbird, MDN Web Docs, and WebAssembly to support developers and users. Through initiatives like Mozilla Foundation, it advocates for digital rights, internet health, and ethical AI.Red panda picture
